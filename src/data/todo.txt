James Robson, Christ in Islam (A. 51, M. 53.)

--------------------
A., 34; M., 36.--
And he said,
I have overturned the world for you on its face, so do not raise it up after me. For part of the wickedness of the world is that disobedience to God is in it; and part of the wickedness of the world is that the next world is attained only by abandoning it. Therefore pass through the world, but do not stay in it; and know that the root of all sin is the love of the world.

Preovrnuo sam svet za vas naglavačke, zato ga ne dižite za mnom. Jer, deo zloće sveta je da je neposlušnost bogu u njemu. A deo zloće sveta je da se sledeći svet postiže samo napuštanjem njega. Zato prođite kroz svet, ali ne ostajte u njemu; i znajte da je koren svih greha ljubav prema svetu.

A., 35; M., 39.--
Isus reče,
The love of this world and of the next cannot stay in the heart of a believer, just as water and fire cannot stay in one vessel.

Ljubav prema ovom svetu i prema sledećem ne može ostati u srcu verujućeg, kao što voda i vatra ne mogu ostati u jednoj posudi.

A., 36; M., 40.--
Someone said to Jesus,
If you were to take a house to cover you [it would be good].
He replied,
The rags of those who lived before us are sufficient for us.

Neko je rekao Isusu:
Ako bi uzeo kuću da vas pokriva [bilo bi dobro].
On je odgovorio,
Krpe onih koji su živeli pre nas su nam dovoljne.

A., 38; M., 42.--
Isus reče,
Woe to him who loves the world! How can he die and leave it and what is in it! It deceives him, yet he trusts in it and puts his confidence in it and he is taken away.
(Woe to the possessor of this world, since he must die and leave the world behind. He places his hope in it and is deceived. He trusts it and is forsaken.)

Jao onome ko voli svet! Kako će umreti i ostaviti svet i šta je u njemu! Svet ga obmanu, ali on poverova i to mu se oduze.
(Jao posedniku na ovom svetu, jer mora umreti i ostaviti svet iza sebe. On položi nadu u to i prevaren je. On verova u to i napušten je.)

And woe to those who are deceived! How what they abhor has caused dissension among them, and what they like has left them, and what they were threatened with has come to them!

Jao onima koji su prevareni! Ono što mrze izazva razdor među njima, i ono što ljube ih napusti, i što im je pretilo sustiže ih!

And woe to him whose care is the world, and whose work is sins!

Jao onome ko brine za svet i čije su delo grijesi!

A., 40; M., 43.--
Who is he who builds a house on the wave of the sea? The world is [like] that, so do not take it as an abiding place.

Isus reče,
Ko još gradi kuću na valima mora? Svet je takav, zato ga nemojte uzimati kao postojano mesto.

A., 41; M., 44.--
Some people said to Jesus:
Teach us one piece of knowledge for which God will love us.
He replied,
Hate the world, and God will love you.

Neki ljudi su rekli Isusu:
Nauči nas jednom deliću znanja zbog koga će nas Bog ljubiti.
On odgovori:
Mrzite svet i Bog će vas voljeti.

A., 42; M., 45.--
Isus reče,
Be pleased with what is worthless in the world along with welfare in religion, just as the people of the world are pleased with what is worthless in religion along with welfare in the world.

Budite zadovoljni bezvrednim u svetu i blagostanjem u veri, kao što su ljudi sveta zadovoljni bezvrednim u veri i blagostanjem u svetu.

A., 43; M., 46.--
O you who seek the world to be charitable with it, your leaving of it alone is more charitable.

O vi koji tražite svet da bude milosrdni sa njim, vaše napuštanje njega je milosrdnije.

The least thing is such that looking after it occupies one to the exclusion of glorifying God, and glorifying God is greater and more important.

Najmanja stvar je takva da briga o njoj zaposeda nekog do isključenja slavljenja Boga, a slavljenje Boga je veće i važnije.

A., 45; M., 48.--
The world was revealed to Jesus and he saw it in the form of a toothless old woman upon whom was every kind of ornament. Then he said to her,
How many have you married?
She replied,
I cannot count them.
He said,
Did they all die and leave you, or did they all divorce you?
She said,
Nay, I killed them all.
Then Isus reče,
It is a pity for the rest of your husbands. How do they not take warning from your past husbands how you have been destroying them one after another, and [how] are they not on their guard against you!

Svet se objavi Isusu u obliku bezube starice na kojoj je bio svaki oblik ukrasa. On joj reče:
Koliko ste se udavali?
Ona odgovori:
Ne mogu ih prebrojati.
On reče:
Da li su svi umrli i napustili vas, ili su se razveli od vas?
Ona reče:
Ne, sve sam ih ubila.
Onda Isus reče:
Šteta za sve vaše muževe. Kako nisu na oprezu zbog vaših prethodnih muževa koje uništiste i kako nisu na straži protiv vas!

A., 47; M., 49.--
Isus reče,
Verily I say unto you, as a sick man looks at food and takes no delight in it because of the severity of the pain, so he who loves the world takes no delight in worship and does not discover its sweetness.

Zaista vam kažem, kako bolestan gleda hranu i ne uživa u njoj zbog jačine bola, tako da onaj ko voli svet ne uživa u obožavanju i ne otkriva njegovu slatkoću.

And verily I say unto you, if a riding-beast is not ridden and taken into service, it becomes intractable and its nature changes; similarly when hearts are not softened by the thought of death and the discipline of worship, they become hard and coarse.

Zaista vam kažem: ako jahaća zver ne bude jahana i primljena u službu, postaje neposlušna i njena priroda se menja. Slično, kada srca nisu omekšana mislima o smrti i obožavanjem, ona postaju tvrda i gruba.

A., 48; M., 50.--
Isus reče,
He who seeks after the world is like one who drinks sea-water; the more he drinks the more his thirst increases, until it kills him.

Onaj ko ište svet je poput onoga koji pije morsku vodu. Što više pije, više se povećava žeđ, sve dok ga ne ubije.

A., 50; M., 52.--
In wealth there are calamities: that one should get it unlawfully.
Someone interrupted,
Suppose one gets it lawfully?
Then he said,
He may apply it unlawfully.
The other said,
But suppose he applies it lawfully?
Then he said,
Its management occupies him to the neglect of God.

Isus reče:
Bogatstvo nosi nesreću, jer stiče se nezakonito.
Neko ga prekinu:
Pretpostavimo da ga neko stekne zakonito?
On tada reče:
Može ga primeniti nezakonito.
Drugi reče:
Ali pretpostavimo da ga zakonito primenjuje?
On tada reče:
Upravljanje njime ga zaokuplja da zanemari boga.

A., 60; M., 57.--
Fine clothing is vanity of heart.

Fina odeća je ispraznost srca.

A., 71; M., 62.--
Jesus passed in his wandering a man asleep wrapped up in his cloak; then he wakened him and said,
O sleeper, arise and glorify God.
Then the man said,
What do you want from me? Verily I have abandoned the world to its people.
So he said to him,
Sleep, then, my friend.

Isus prođe pored čoveka koji spavaše umotan u ogrtač. On ga probudi i reče:
O spavaču, ustani i proslavi boga!
Tada je čovek rekao:
Šta hoćeš od mene? Zaista sam prepustio svijet njegovim ljudima.
Pa mu reče:
Spavaj, onda, prijatelju.


A., 72.--Jesus said,
With difficulty does a rich man enter heaven.
Teško bogataš moće ući u carstvo.

A., 73; M., 18.--Jesus said,
Verily I love poverty and hate comfort.

Zaista volim siromaštvo i mrzim udobnost.

A., 74; M., 63.--Jesus said,
Do not look at the wealth of the people of this world, for the splendour of their wealth takes away the light of your faith.

Ne gledaj u bogatstvo ljudi ovog sveta, jer sjaj njihovog bogatstva oduzima svetlost tvoje vere.

A., 113.--
It is astonishing how you work for this world, yet you receive provision in it without work; and how you do not work for the next world, yet you receive provision in it only with work.

Zapanjujuće je koliko radite za ovaj svet, a dobijate opskrbu u njemu bez posla. I kako ne radite za naredni svet, a dobijate opskrbu u njemu samo radom.

A., 118 .--
I am he who has inverted the world on its face; so I have no wife to die and no house to be demolished.

Ja sam taj koji je prevrnuo svet naglavačke; pa nemam ženu koja bi umrla i ni kuću koja bi bila srušena.

A., 125.--
Jesus used to say,
The sweetness of this world is the bitterness of the next, and fine clothes are vanity of heart, meaning conceit and pride of heart. And fullness of the belly is abundance of passion, meaning its sustenance and its accumulation.

Slatkoća ovog sveta je gorčina sledećeg. I lepa odeća je ispraznost srca, što znači umišljenost i gordost. I punoća stomaka je obilje strasti, što znači njihovo podržavanje i gomilanje.

A., 126.--
Jesus lifted up a stone and asked,
Which of the two is dearer to you, this or a dînâr and a dirhem?
They replied,
A dînâr.
He said,
They are both alike to me.

Isus je podigao kamen i dinar i pitao:
Šta vam je od ova dva draže?
Oni su odgovorili:
A dinar.
On reče:
Meni su oba isti.

A., 220.--
Which of them is sweeter to your hearts?
They said,
The gold.
He said,
They are both alike to me.

Isus uze zemlju u jednu ruku i zlato u drugu. Tada upita drugove:
Koji je od njih slađi vašem srcu?
Rekli su,
Zlato.
Rekao je,
Oboje su mi slični.

A., 193.--
Know that the relation of your present life to your future life is like the relation of the east to the west. The more you approach the east, the farther you are from the west; and the more you approach the west, the farther do you increase in distance from the east.

Znajte da je odnos vašeg sadašnjeg života prema vašem budućem životu sličan odnosu istoka prema zapadu. Što više pristupate istoku, to ste dalje od zapada; i što više pristupate zapadu, to se dalje povećava udaljenost od istoka.

M., 19. There is no poor man poorer than I.
Nema siromaha siromašnijeg od mene.

A., 53; M., 54.
O you learned men who are evil, you fast and pray and give alms, and do not do what you are commanded, and you teach what you do not perform.

O vi učeni ljudi koji ste zli, postite, molite se i dajete milostinju, i ne radite ono što vam je naređeno, i učite ono što ne činite.

What does it avail you to purify your skins while your hearts are filthy? Verily I say unto you, do not be like a sieve from which the good flour goes out and in which the siftings remain. Similarly you utter the decree from your mouths, while malice remains in your hearts.

Šta vam koristi da pročistite svoju kožu dok su vam prljava srca? Zaista vam kažem, ne budite poput sita iz kojih dobro brašno izlazi i u kojem ostaje prosijavanje. Slično, izgovarate dekrete iz svojih usta, dok zloba ostaje u vašim srcima.

Woe to you! What does it avail a dark house if a lamp is placed on its roof when inside is black darkness? Similarly, it will not avail you that the light of knowledge should be in your mouths when your inner parts are waste and uncultivated.

Teško vama! Šta koristi tamnoj kući ako je na njenom krovu lampa, kad je unutra mrkli mrak? Slično, neće vam koristiti svetlost znanja u vašim ustima kad je unutar vas pustoš.

O slaves of the world, you are not pious like slaves, or honourable like freemen.

O robovi sveta, niste pobožni kao robovi, niti časni poput slobodnjaka.

A., 61; M., 58. Isus reče,
Why do you come to me wearing monks' clothing while your hearts are the hearts of ravenous wolves? Put on king's clothing and mortify your hearts with fear.

Zašto dolazite kod mene noseći monašku odeću dok su vam srca besnih vukova? Bolje obucite kraljevu odeću i umrtvite svoja srca strahom.

A., 161.--
Jesus, son of Mary said,
Be ashamed before God in your secret affairs, as you are ashamed before Him in your public affairs.

Sramite se pred bogom u svojim tajnim poslovima, kao što se sramite u javnim.

A., 197.--
Isus reče,
O jurists, you have sat on the road of the life to come, but you have not walked so that you might reach it, nor have you allowed anyone to pass you to it.

O pravnici, sedite na putu narednog života, ali ne hodate da biste ga dostigli, niti dozvoljavate da neko prođe.

A., 216.
Woe to you, slaves of the world! What use is the spreading of the sun's light to a blind man when he does not see it? Similarly the abundance of a learned man's knowledge is of no use to him when he does not act according to it.

Jao vama, robovi sveta! Kakva je korist od širenja sunčeve svetlosti na slepog čoveka kada ga ne vidi? Slično, obilje znanja učenog čoveka mu ništa ne koristi kada ne postupa u skladu s tim.

How many fruits of trees are there, yet all of them are not useful and are not eaten! And how many learned men are there, yet all of them do not profit from what they learn!

Koliko ima plodova drveća, ali svi nisu korisni i ne jedu se! I koliko učenih ljudi postoji, koji nemaju korist od onoga što uče!

So be on your guard against the false learned men who wear woollen clothing and bend down their heads to the earth, glancing under their eyebrows as wolves glance.

Pazite se učenjaka koji nose vunenu odeću i klanjaju se glavama ka zemlji, gledajući ispod svojih obrva kao vukovi.

Their speech disagrees with their action. Who plucks grapes from thorns and figs from colocynth? Similarly the speech of a false learned man produces only falsehood.

Njihov govor se ne slaže sa njihovim delima. Ko bere grožđe sa trnja i smokve sa kolocinta? Slično, govor lažnog učenjaka proizvodi samo laž.

Hamza Yusuf, Walk on Water: The Wisdom of Jesus from Traditional Arabic Sources
---------
Beware of the luxuries of this world, and fear them, for the luxuries of this world are filth in the sight of God.

Čuvajte raskoši ovog sveta i plašite ih se, jer su raskoši ovog sveta prljavština pred bogom.

Love of this world is the source of every wrong action, and there is much harm in wealth.
They asked:
“What is its harm?”
Jesus replied:
“Its possessor is never safe from pride and arrogance.”
They said:
“What if he is free of those two? Is there still harm?”
Jesus responded:
“Yes, for by nurturing his wealth, he is diverted from the remembrance of God.”

Isus reče:
Ljubav prema svetu je izvor svakog pogrešnog dela i ima mnogo štete u bogatstvu.
Pitali su:
Šta je šteta?
Isus odgovori:
Njegov posjednik nikada nije bezbedan od gordosti i oholosti.
Rekli su:
Šta ako je slobodan od ova dva? Ima li još štete?
Isus odgovori:
Da, jer se negujući svoje bogatstvo, odvraća se od sećanja na Boga.
